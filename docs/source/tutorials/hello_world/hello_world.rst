Hello World
===========

Welcome to the OpenMETA Hello World tutorial. You are about to create your first
OpenMETA project! Through this tutorial you will be exposed to some of the basic
modeling concepts of OpenMETA as you model the organizational structure of your
company.

We will cover only what is needed to complete the present exercise, but links
will often be made to the reference section of the documentation where you can
go for a deeper understanding and explanation of the covered topics.

Let's begin!
